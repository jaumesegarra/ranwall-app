What's that?

Are you tired of your bored wallpaper 😑?

ranwall is an app for discover new wallpapers just doing a click 😮, if you liked some you can set it to your desktop with an other click. EASY 🌄!

Usage?

Launch the app and see the random wallpaper, if you liked it click on 'set' button; if you liked it so much click on download ⬇️ icon to save as image.

Oops you don't like it, well click on refresh button to try with another wallpaper.
